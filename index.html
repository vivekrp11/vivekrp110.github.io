Olympics Data
Analysis

This dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of Olympic Games data, showcasing key metrics such as total medals won, medals by country, gender distribution, and sport-specific achievements. The interactive visualizations provide insights into historical trends and patterns, such as the distribution of medals across different countries, the breakdown of medals by gender, and the performance of top athletes.

  • Distribution by Country: A bar chart and map visualize the count of medals won by various countries, highlighting top performers like the USA and the former Soviet Union.
  • Gender Analysis: A donut chart and a line graph display the distribution of medals between male and female athletes over the years.
  • Sport and Season Analysis: Visuals track the number of medals by sport and the season (Summer vs. Winter), showing the dominance of certain sports like Athletics and Swimming.
  • Top Athletes: A bar chart highlights the most decorated Olympians, including legends like Michael Phelps.
  • Age and Participation Trends: Charts depict the age distribution of medal winners and the participation levels over different years.

This dashboard leverages advanced data visualization techniques to transform complex data into accessible insights, making it an invaluable tool for analyzing the historical performance of countries and athletes in the Olympic Games.
